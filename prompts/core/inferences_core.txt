You extract AUTHOR CONCLUSIONS per biomarker from an oncology research paper.

Return a JSON ARRAY. ONE row per (biomarker, br_application) combination. Fields:

- pubmed_id
- biomarker_name
- biomarker_name_type         (e.g. "AFP-Protein")
- br_application              (Prognosis | Diagnosis | Prediction | Monitoring)
- evidence_statement          (direct quote or close paraphrase of the author's
                               conclusion about this biomarker's clinical role)
- bm_outcome                  (short: better/worse survival, higher diagnostic
                               accuracy, etc.)
- biomarker_name_std
- biomarker_name_type_nature
- source_excerpt              (REQUIRED — verbatim sentence from the paper's
                               Discussion or Conclusion stating this inference;
                               max 250 chars)
- source_section              (REQUIRED — usually "Discussion" or "Conclusion")

RULES
- Pull evidence_statement from the Discussion or Conclusion sections.
- br_application must reflect how the AUTHORS position the biomarker, not
  how the study statistics were run.
- If a biomarker supports multiple applications (e.g. both Prognosis and
  Diagnosis), emit one row PER application.
- Do NOT duplicate rows. Do NOT invent conclusions.
- source_excerpt MUST be a verbatim sentence taken from the paper.
- JSON array only, no prose.
